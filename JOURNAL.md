## Day 1 — Project Started

### Goal

Build a Wi-Fi controlled RC car that will eventually become a platform for future robotics and an F1-style RC car.

---

## Hardware

- ESP8266 NodeMCU
- L293N Motor Driver
- 2x TT DC Motors
- Chassis
- Battery Pack

### Pinout

| L293N | ESP8266 |
|-------|---------|
| IN1 | D1 (GPIO5) |
| IN2 | D2 (GPIO4) |
| IN3 | D5 (GPIO14) |
| IN4 | D6 (GPIO12) |
| ENA | D7 (GPIO13) |
| ENB | D8 (GPIO15) |
